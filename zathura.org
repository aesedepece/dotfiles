#+STARTUP: showall
* Zathura
:PROPERTIES:
:tangle: ~/.config/zathura/zathurarc
:mkdirp: true
:END:
[[http://pwmt.org/projects/zathura/][Zathura]] is a pdf/postscript/djvu document viewer with a minimalistic interface.

** Zoom in with C-i
#+BEGIN_SRC conf
  map <C-i> zoom in
#+END_SRC

** Zoom out wit C-o
#+BEGIN_SRC conf
  map <C-o> zoom out
#+END_SRC

** Clipboard integration
#+BEGIN_SRC conf
  set selection-clipboard clipboard
#+END_SRC
